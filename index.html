About.

You may have discovered this website by accident or you may have come here through one of my social media channels. In any case, I wish you a warm welcome to my website.

I’m Alex or more commonly known as Alexmoenz. When I was 9 years old I held a camera for the first time and since then I can't live without it. Maybe I’m addicted to photography?

My focus is mainly centered on lifestyle and landscape photography but I'm always keen to try new types of photography. For me photography is a cooperation between composition, light and subject. It’s a perfect balance only masters can achieve. With that said I would say, grab a beer (or a still water in case you don’t drink alcohol) and enjoy!
